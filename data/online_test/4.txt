Change your verizon number online.
Go to VerizonWireless.com.
Log in as the account owner.  
Use your username and password to access your account information. 
If someone else deals with the account, you'll need to get the log-in info from them. 
Look for the MyPhone actions column.  
After you click MyPhone, the column will be to the left.
Below, you'll see a link to change your cell phone number. 
Walk through the steps.  The steps will guide you through the process of changing your number. 
Once you've finished the process, you'll need to dial *228 and select option 1 to reprogram your phone.
